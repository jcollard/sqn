Space Quest 5 Goliath Maps
A few months ago, I talked to this alien stranger in my favorite bar: the Magmetheus Canteen. He was trying to sell some hot maps of StarCon's biggest starships. Among the various secret maps (which must have been stolen from some HQ) were the tunnel maps of the Goliath. So, naturally, I laid down a handful of buckazoids (and an Astro Chicken Flight Hat) and called it a deal. I'm prepared to share these maps exclusively with Space Quest fans, so that nobody has to get stuck in the Goliath tunnel system ever again.

A note regarding all Goliath maze maps: From any location other than the elevator shafts, there is access to the engineering area corridor above. All of these corridor screens are dead ends.
