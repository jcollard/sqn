>> Miscellaneous >> Buying the Games

I get weekly mails from people who want to buy Space Quest. I've therefore created this article which helps you finding, and eventually buying the Space Quest games.

Sierra stopped selling the SQ games somewhere in 1998 if I remember correctly. At the same time they stopped their technical support for that product (except some poor patches in the download section). What's worse, Sierra stopped supplying the manual to people who've lost theirs. This means they've completely abandoned Space Quest. If you're looking for the games, for help or technical support, asking Sierra is as useless as trying to light water with a pocket knife. At the moment, there's no reason to believe they're going to sell the games again. Sierra has stated reprinting the collection box and its manual is too expensive and will only lose them money as there isn't enough demand for the product. If they are ever to resume production (fat chance), Amazon.com is the first place to check as they handle Sierra's online sales. The collection (although out of stock and discontinued) is set to $20 US.
The Space Quest games are very hard to find, but it's not impossible. I always recommend people towww.Ebay.com, the largest online auction site in the world. If you search for "Space Quest" you'll get a list with offered games. If you can't find your Space Quest, don't worry. Return some days later and try again. It's the perfect place to get this kind of games. The collections are sold for enormous amounts of money, frequently selling wel over 100 bucks. I therefore recommend buying the games seperately, unless you live overseas and have to pay for the rediculous shipping costs. Sign up for your own account and start bidding. If you bid on people who have over 20 postive feedbacks, things can hardly go wrong. All my transactions at Ebay went great, and I've done quite a few. I've bought my entire Space Quest Collection at Ebay, and that's saying something. For payment I recommend using PayPal.com. Go to that site and register. Please note that the seller at Ebay must accept paypal of course . Online payments will be easy, fast and secure. You only need to enter your creditcard info once.
While you're at it, please donate some money for my site
Another possible way to get Space Quest is by looking through the bargain bins of software stores. You never know what's hidden inside these bins'o'pleasure. I've bough several Space Quest Collections and Space Quest 6 this way. It's always worth the try.
