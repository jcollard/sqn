If you have a Space Quest or Sierra related site and would like to see your link posted here than mail me.

 

Roger Wilco's Virtual Broomcloset
 The first Space Quest site on the web. Unlike , The Virtual Broomcloset is more or less more centred around the "fan" part of the community, such as fangames, fanprojects and fanfiction. It contains all the top-notch stuff you should expect from a Space Quest Fansites such as hints, downloads and too-cool interviews. Take me there!

 

The Many Deaths of Roger Wilco
 A website with a very original concept, featuring just about every way to die in the Space Quest Series. Get shot, stepped on, deleted, turned inside out, squashed, burned, decapitated, exploded, shocked, decompressed, puked, terminated, melted, eaten alive, blasted, and twisted: This site knows how to get the best out of Roger's physical abilities (and just a bit more). I certainly can't think of one dead Jeysie has missed. And that's saying something. As a bonus, this website also contains the deaths from the fanprojects. Take me there!

 

The Other Side of Space Quest
 A fansite with a rather intriguing twist. It's not really about Space Quest, but about people, places or objects that share the name Space Quest, Roger Wilco, or any other Space Questy terms, but have no direct relation to the actual SQ game series. Take me there!

 

Sq7.Org
 Home of the Space Quest 7 fanproject. Made by fans, for the fans. Be sure to check out their latest Status Release! Take me there!

 

SQFanGames
 This website is a complete database with information about every Space Quest fanproject, whether it's released, in production or cancelled. Check out their download section for fangames or demo's that are available. Take me there!

The Official Space Quest FAQ
 The webmaster has made it possible to view the famous SQFAQ in HTML format. Take me there!

 

Space Quest -2: The Xenonian Corruption Fanproject
 Home of the Space Quest Prequels project. Danny Bloks and his team are working on a game that takes place before the original Space Quest 1. They are pretty far in production and are close to releasing a beta. Take me there!

Space Quest: Vohaul Strikes Back Fanproject
 This is yet another fanproject that shows potential of being released. Their screenshots look rather nice! Take me there!

 

The StarCon Website
This is an awesome website made by Space Quest Fan Nicolas Cross back in 2000. It's a shame it isn't updated anymore, although Nicolas is still considered to be an active member of the community. In my humble opinion, this fansite is one of the best ever made. Take me there!

 

The Golden Mop Society
This is a fansite from the very early days. It hasn't been updated since February 26, 1999 but it still is an awesome fansite. The webmaster disappeared into thin air after Space Quest 7 got cancelled. Take me there!
