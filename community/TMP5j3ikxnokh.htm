The Janitorial Times
This is the message board of . You're more than welcome to post a message if need a game hint or technical support. Or, if you would just like to talk about Space Quest! Guest posts are allowed at the Janitorial Times, no need to register. Take me there!

The SubSpace Channel
This is the message board of the Virtual Broomcloset. It's considered to be the nerve centre of the Space Quest Universe. You'll find many different people there, from die-hard fans (who are there much longer than is considered healthy) to newbies with questions how to get past the Endodroid Runner. It has the infamous "Off-Topic Board" where Space Quest fans talk about every day life (should they have one) and discuss just about everything you can imagine. Take me there!

The Space Quest Chatroom
The Space Quest Universe frequently gathers via Internet Relay Chat (IRC), to discuss such important issues as Roger's women, Sierra, or if pancakes are better with syrup or honey. Frequently mistaken for "#SQ", this chatroom has dangerous side-effects and is known to make you a social outcast in real-life. To join in the fun and silliness join the room "#sq" on server "irc.freenode.net". You should download an IRC client, such as mIRC , or use the web gateway on Sq7.Org.

Space Quest and Sierra Links
The Space Quest Universe stretches out deep into Cyberspace. From alive and frequently updated websites, to the "golden oldies" whose webmasters have stopped updating ever since Space Quest 7 got cancelled. Find links to "must see" Space Quest and Sierra fansites. Take me there!
