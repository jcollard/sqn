Adventure Comics (a division of Malibu Graphics Publishing Group) released three comic books in 1992 (#1 - February 1992, #2 - April 1992, #3 - May 1992) called "The adventures of Roger Wilco" based upon the popular Space Quest computer game. The first issue was released in full color and was written by John Shaw, while the other two were released in black and white and are written by Paul O'Connor. The print run was very small, so these three comic books are very hard to find.Wonder why the first issue was released in full color, while the other two are in black and white? Malibu Graphics had plans to release all three issues in colour. They probably trashed that idea because part one didn't sell that good...

Please note that the comic books have some advertisement pages but I left those out.
