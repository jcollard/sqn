Space Quest 4 Demos
Sierra never released a (non) playable demo for Space Quest 4 in the form of a DOS program. They did release Ms Astro Chicken as stand-alone game. Later, Sierra released "Nick's Picks" around 1993, which included three arcade sequences from the VGA Space Quest games.

Nick's Picks (442 KB)
Contains Ms. Astro Chicken, the skimmer sequence and the Monolith Burger arcade game.

Ms Astro Chicken (340 KB)
The sturdy wife of Astro Chicken needs your help! Go cluck it!
