SQN will be back soon

Nijmegen the Netherlands, May 12, 2005

Dear visitor,

The Entertainment Software Association, or ESA for short, has requested the immediate removal of SpaceQuest.Net. Apparently, this proud institution is after your favorite fansite, that has been online tirelessly for the past 6.5 years to promote Space Quest.

We're planning a petition to persuade Sierra/Vivendi to "officialize" or recognize SpaceQuest.Net. Please check back later for the latest news.

You can stay up to date by visiting our message board. It contains a special ESA VS. SQN section. You can post your feelings and encouragements there. At least these ESA people can't take away our freedom of speech.

Please do not contact me personally about this matter by mail, MSN or ICQ. Help us fight, join us!

Kind regards,

Frans van Hofwegen
