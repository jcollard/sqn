Space Piston
This small booklet came with the original release of Space Quest 2. It contains a manual insert and a very funny comic that gives us an inside about Roger's life after Space Quest 1 and just before Space Quest 2. Its dimensions are just 13cm. wide and 17cm. high. This is a pretty rare Space Quest item, I might say.

I've scanned and edited these files with great care. It was very hard to preserve all the details, which are already very hard to see even in the original booklet.
