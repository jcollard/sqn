Space Quest 6 Point List
You can get a maximum score of 500 points with Space Quest 6. However, there's a bug in the game that enables you to get even more! When inside Stellar's stomach, you can retrieve secretion from the Isles of Langerhans, return to the stomach, pour the contents over the pill, then return and get more - and you get three points for it every time! So, in theory, you can get as many points as you want.
