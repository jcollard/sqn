Roger Wilco's Virtual Broomcloset
 This is the first Space Quest fansite on the internet. Unlike , The Virtual Broomcloset is more or less centred around the "fan" part of the community, such as fangames, fanprojects and fanfiction. It contains all the top-notch stuff you should expect from a Space Quest fansite such as hints, downloads and too-cool interviews. Take me there!

The Many Deaths of Roger Wilco
 A website with a very original concept, featuring just about every way to die in the Space Quest Series. Get shot, squashed, burned, decapitated, exploded, stepped on, deleted, turned inside out, shocked, decompressed, shattered, puked, terminated, melted, eaten alive, blasted, twisted and more. This site sure knows how to get the best out Roger's physical abilities (plus a bit more than he can handle). I certainly can't think of one dead Jeysie has missed. And that's saying something. As a bonus, this website also contains the deaths from fangames. Take me there!

The Other Side of Space Quest
 A fansite with a rather intriguing twist. It's not really about Space Quest, but about people, places and objects that share the name Space Quest, Roger Wilco, or any other Space Questy terms, but have no direct relation to the actual Series. Take me there!

SQFanGames
 This website is a complete database containing information about every Space Quest fanproject (released, in production or cancelled). Check out their download section for fangames or demo's. Take me there!

Who Says Space Quest Is Dead?
 Akril's website features comics based after Sierra On-Line's presumably cancelled Space Quest 7. The website has been around for quite a while and is still being updated frequently. Take me there!

Sq7.Org (fangame project)
Home of the Space Quest 7 fanproject. Made by fans, for the fans. Be sure to check out their latest Status Release! Take me there!

Space Quest: Vohaul Strikes Back (fangame project)
 This is a fanproject that shows potential of being released. Their screenshots look rather nice! Take me there!

Space Quest III: VGA Remake (fangame project)
 Though their website looks a bit outdated, this project is still alive and kicking! Khaveen & co are trying to release SQ# with new VGA graphics and an icon interface. Go check out their demo! Take me there!

The Official Space Quest FAQ
 The webmaster of this site has made it possible to view the famous SQFAQ in HTML format. Take me there!

The StarCon Website
This is an awesome website made by Space Quest Fan Nicolas Cross back in 2000. It's a shame it isn't updated anymore, although Nicolas himself is still considered to be an active member of the community. In my humble opinion, this fansite is one of the best ever made. Take me there!

The Space Quest Collection
Yet another Nicolas Cross product! This is the first 100% Flash SQ fansite ever. Take me there!

 

Sierra always had and has a very large amount of fanssites supporting those old time favorites. But there's more to Sierra than just Space Quest. The following links are the most worthwhile Sierra fansites out there.

AGI Development Site - Tons of information about the AGI engine. Take me there!

Al Lowe's Humor Site - Yikes, Al Lowe somehow survived it all. Take me there!

BriPro.com - Home of the SCI Studio game development system. Take me there!

Chris' AGI Website - Your AGI resource page! Take me there!

Faux INN - A fanproject that has re-created the old INN / TSN. Take me there!

FreddyPharkas.com - THE best fansite about this game made by Al Lowe. Take me there!

Good Old Adventures - A chatsite where you can chat in your favorite AGI game. Take me there!

LarryLives.com - News about the new Leisure Suit Larry for PC, XBox and PS2. Take me there!

Quest for More Glory - This is a cool fansite about Quest for Glory. Take me there!

Quest for Glory Series - Another cool QfG fansite. Take me there!

Quest Studios - Covers music from Sierra games. Take me there!

Sierra Classics - Offers the Sierra Oldies for download. Take me there!

SierraGamers.com - Ken Williams himself runs this website. Take me there!

Sierra Music Centre - Like Quest Studios, this website is about Sierra music. Only better. Take me there!

Sierra's Past - A cool website featuring animated gifs from many Sierra Adventures! Take me there!

Sierra Planet - Mainly deals with games made by Roberta Williams. Take me there

Tierra Entertainment - Is remaking dated Sierra games into dated VGA graphics. Take me there

Ugarte's Dimension of Fantasy - A fansite that worships old Sierra games. Take me there!

Vintage Sierra - The Collector's Resource! Take me there!
