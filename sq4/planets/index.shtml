Space Quest 4 Planets > Roger visits a few interesting planets during his fourth quest. Please keep in mind that you might not want to see the information below if you're new to the game.


Magmetheus
This planet is colonized by Xenonians and just a stone throw away from Xenon. It is the home of the Space Bar, one of the most famous and spaciest Space Bars in the universe. Although there are many inhabitants on this planet, it is mainly used as a last stop for space travelers. It offers a last chance to refuel and resupply before going out of Earnon.

Xenon
Roger's home planet. It's been awhile since Roger saw Xenon for the last time. It's the home for 9 billion human-like-beings. In the Space Quest 12 era, Xenon is nothing but a pile. The Xenon Super Computer which controlled weather and defense systems turned against the Xenonians.... But there is more to you than meets the eye.

Kerona
Roger didn't have the slightest idea what he was doing when he punched in those codes he found in that old gumrap and hint book. Well, he would soon find out, as he was about to travel back to the 16 coloured Ulence Flats! The city looks different somehow, less crowed. That's easy to explain though; the summer season is over and everybody went to visit other places. Nobody wants to be around when those dreadful sandstorms hit the city! Though the bar appears to be open for business...

Estros
You've seen a lot of plateaus and sloping hills before, but this one's a butte. The entire horizon undulates in enticing peaks, curves and crevices for which Estros is famous. The hot sun and the butte's seductive curves are strangely exciting. Beware the "tranquil" Sea of Estros... Wait until you'll find the Latex Babes! The picture of the planet was taken by Nicholas Cross, that universally famous photographer.
