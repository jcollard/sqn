A last word

Before shouting at me about copyrights, read the following. Sierra stopped selling the games back in 1997 and stopped offering support, game hints and replacement manuals. This means from that point on Space Quest fans only have each other to turn to. The amount of circulating copies are rapidly dying into obscurity. Sierra has abandoned the games and showed no interest what so ever in selling them in the future. Where are fans and players to turn too? Not Sierra, that's pretty obvious. My intent was and IS never to hurt Sierra. And I don't think I do. I'm not losing them any money. Nor am I putting them in a bad light. Quite on the contrary.

If you disagree with the statement above and can't see what positive impact my website might have on fans, adventure game lovers, gaming enthusiasts and Sierra than that's no more but your loss. will continue its ever on-going battle to preserve Space Quest.
