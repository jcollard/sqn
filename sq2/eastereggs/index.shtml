Space Quest 2 Easter Eggs > An "easter egg" is a hidden feature that is intended to be found by the player. Easter eggs started out as a method of rebellion by early game programmers who were not allowed to take credit for their work.


Space Quest 2 Easter Eggs

While playing, type "cheat" and press return. This will bring you to an alternative "the end" screen, with 255 points out of 250. This works with version 2.0F. A classical Two Guys Easter Egg :)

You can "take condom" in front of your locker of the XOS 4. It will say: "Sorry. The used Nancy Reagan autographed "Just Say No" condom had to be removed for the shipping version."

After you've crash-landed on Labion, type "fuck dead body". The game will output: "You're one twisted mofo. The only other person I know who would try that is Mark Crowe". Obviously, Scott Murphy programmed this into the game, unknowingly to Mark.
